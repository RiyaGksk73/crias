Feature Engineering

The following features are computed from raw financial data:

Feature Formula Interpretation
Current Ratio assets / debt Higher is better. Measures ability to pay short-term obligations.
Debt Ratio debt / assets Lower is better. Proportion of assets financed by debt.
Liquidity Ratio cash / debt Higher is better. Ability to pay debt with liquid assets.

RCES Formula

Risk-Cost-Effectiveness Score ranks counterfactual strategies:

RCES = (PD_old - PD_new) / (cost + 1)


Cost = |Δinventory| × 0.001 + |Δcash| × 0.001 + |Δdebt| × 0.002

Higher RCES indicates more cost-effective risk reduction.
